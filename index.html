Aradhana

Aradhana is a three day cultural fest organised by SPICMACAY NITK chapter which seeks to increase the awareness about living culture and heritage of India accessible to the earth by organising various workshop and encourages the students by giving them a great platform to showcase the talents and efforts.

Intro

SPICMACAY is a voluntary youth movement which promotes the intangible aspects of Indian cultural heritage by promoting classical music, classical dance, folk arts, yoga, crafts and other aspects of Indian Culture throughout the world. It is a movement with chapters in over more than 300 towns all over the world.

Call for Sponsors

ARADHANA is an opportunity for sponsors to help us - The SPICMACAY Volunteers, promote an awareness of the rich and heterogeneous cultural tapestry among students, by organising various workshops, lecture demonstrations and competitions. ARADHANA also features performances by talented artists during these days and will require significant efforts. Being A Premiere National Institute, NITK will be able to reach out to a large audience through ARADHANA. Funding to help ensure the best quality of the fest would be much appreciated.
